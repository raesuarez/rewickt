about rewickt

After 8 years of doing homework and thinking about doing homework, I have finally earned my bachelor's degree. Now that it's useless, I can spend my days consuming media! Hooray!

I have a job now and I'm making it your problem. I am posting reviews of all the books, movies, games, and shows I watch now that I can enjoy them guilt-free. I am catching up on all of the titles I missed trying to be a try hard so don't be shocked when I review titles that are several years old. They are new to me!
